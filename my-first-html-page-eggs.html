The Perfect Sunny-Side-Up Eggs

Enjoy perfect fried eggs for breakfast, lunch or dinner. This recipe is easy to make and tastes great!

Prep time: 5 min. Serves: 1

How to make Sunny-Side-Up Eggs?

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Get a nonstick frying pan on a medium heat
  2. Add enough olive oil to cover the pan
  3. Crack the eggs in to the pan
  4. Cook until the tops of the whites are set but the yolks are still runny
  5. Serve hot and season with salt
